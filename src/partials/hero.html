Still Corner: Hidden Patterns

It's this puzzle game where you're looking for things that are literally right in front of you, but somehow you just... don't see them at first.

Mira’s note: “If you’re expecting loud colors and fast moves… you’re in the wrong place.
Here, you just look. And look again.”

A calm street corner. A wall. A window reflection.
Nothing special—until you tilt the view and a hidden structure begins to appear.

What you do: rotate the scene, shift perspective, find the alignment that reveals the pattern.
Mira’s note:
“I don’t know why… but in Still Corner, everything eventually folds into a design.”
