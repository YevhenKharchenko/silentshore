From gentle hints to deep structures

  • Early levels:

    • simple patterns

    • strong visual cues

    • wide tolerance

  • Mid levels:

    • multi-layer geometry

    • reflections and overlapping lines

    • partial structures you must “complete” mentally

  • Advanced levels:

    • near-invisible alignments

    • pattern rules that evolve

    • minimal hints

    • tighter precision requirements

Still calm. Still minimal. Just more subtle, more demanding.
